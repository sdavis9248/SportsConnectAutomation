# Schedules & Season — Region 58 (Fall 2026)

## Season Timing [VERIFY specifics]
- **Fall 2026** runs approximately **September–December 2026** (some sources: late August start).
- **Divisions up to 14U:** begin **late August 2026**, end ~**November 2026**. Season start commonly cited ~**Sept 19**.
- **16U and 19U:** begin **mid-August 2026**, end **early November 2026** — a shortened **~6-week** season to avoid conflicts with high school soccer.
- **No regular games the weekend after Thanksgiving** — the **Turkey Tournament** is held that weekend (voluntary, fun-focused).
- **No games on Rosh Hashanah.**

## Postseason (Competitive Divisions) [VERIFY]
- Competitive divisions (10U, 12U, 14U, 16U, 19U) teams may **advance to Area and Section play**.
- **10U/12U/14U postseason:** December 2026; advancing teams may play **January–April 2027**.
- **16U/19U postseason:** late October 2026; advancing teams may play **November 2026–April 2027**.
- (Full All-Star / Tournament eligibility and logistics: see all-star-and-tournament.md.)

## Game & Practice Locations
- **Primary location (14U and younger):** **Van Nuys Sherman Oaks Memorial Park, 14332 Hartsook St., Sherman Oaks, CA 91423.**
- **16U games:** Sundays at **North Valley AYSO Region 174, 12940 Balboa Blvd, Granada Hills, CA 91344** (typically a 9–10 game season).
- **19U:** often plays at other parks.

## Games (14U and Younger)
- **Saturdays**, ~1 hour long, generally scheduled **8:00 AM–3:00 PM**; game times can vary week to week. Plan on **~60–80 minutes** at the park.

## Younger-Division Specifics
- **5U Schoolyard / Jamboree (and younger):** coed; **Saturdays only**, sessions generally scheduled **between 8 AM and noon**, with groups of about **15–20 participants**. **Once a group's session time is set, it stays the same each week** for the season. **No games per se** — participants practice various soccer skills and engage in joint activities.
- **6U:** instructional — practice then game, both on Saturdays (**no weekday practices**); no score-keeping; coaches act as referees.
- **7U and older:** **weekday practices** (coach sets day/time, typically **1–2×/week, 60–90 min**) at the park, plus Saturday games. Assume weekday afternoons.
