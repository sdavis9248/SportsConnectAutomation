# Fees, Payments & Refunds — Region 58 (Fall 2026)

## Current Fees (Fall 2026)
- **Local registration fee: $205 per player.**
- **AYSO National Player Fee (NPF): $25 per player** — non-refundable, charged by AYSO National (not the region), always paid in full.
- **Total: $230 per player.**
- This **superseded** the prior SportsConnect-era pricing of $195 + $25 = $220. (An early internal setup draft listed tiered prices like $85/$115/$125 by age; that draft is **outdated/superseded** by the flat **$205 + $25** pricing confirmed in the live test-parent walkthrough. [VERIFY])

## Discounts
- **Early-bird discount: $30 off — ENDED for the 2026 season. No longer available; do not offer or mention it as available.** (It applied through 11:59 PM Pacific on the early-bird end date earlier in registration.)
- **NO multi-child / sibling / family discount.** Each child pays the full $230 ($205 + $25 NPF). If a parent asks about a discount for registering multiple children, tell them Region 58 does not offer one. (Financial aid is available separately and applies per child — see financial-aid.md.)
- **No late fees. No payment plans.**

## What the NPF Is
- **NPF = National Player Fee** (PlayMetrics term: **"Club Fee"**) — a per-player fee AYSO National assesses on every registered player. Charged **once per player per season**, even across multiple programs.
- The NPF, when assessed, **must always be paid in full** — it cannot be split into installments.

## Payment Methods
- Accepted payment methods include **check** and **Zelle** (for region-handled payments); online registration is processed through the PlayMetrics merchant account (credit card).
- Fees are **seasonal (per-season)**, not monthly.

## Fees Absorbed by the Region
- The region **absorbs** the **credit card processing fee (~2.8%)** and the **order/service fee** — these are NOT passed to parents.
- Statement note: include "you'll see a charge from [legal merchant name]" in confirmations, since the statement descriptor may differ from the region's common name.

## Refunds & Cancellation
- **Cancellation/refund deadline: July 15, 2026.** (One source referenced July 16, 2026 — **[VERIFY]** exact date before quoting.)
- **How to request a refund:** Email **registrar@ayso58.org from the registering account's email**, with the child's full name. No refunds via casual/phone communication.
- **The $25 NPF is NEVER refundable.**
- A **$30 portion is non-refundable** per the refund policy (this includes the $25 non-refundable AYSO National membership fee). [VERIFY exact non-refundable structure]
- **After the deadline:** the region generally still refunds the **registration portion** if a schedule turns out to be unworkable, but the **$25 NPF is never refundable**.
- **No refund** after any team activity participation.

## Refund Policy Mechanics (for reference)
- Refund deadline is tied to a **specific date** (e.g., July 15) rather than "30 days before season" to avoid ambiguity.
- Refund policy text is stored as **text** (not a link) in the merchant account.
- PlayMetrics checkout includes a **built-in refund policy checkbox** — a separate refund-policy waiver is redundant and was removed.

## Refunds & Program Changes (Admin Path)
- To refund: **Programs → 2026 Fall Core → Subscriptions → click player's subscription → cancel/refund options** (same screen as View Program Responses). The **Treasurer/Controller** on the merchant account may need to process refunds.
- **Program Changes** (Club Programs page) is the workflow to **transfer a player between packages/programs** (play-up moves, wrong-division corrections); it handles financial adjustments.
- **Payments cannot be transferred between AYSO regions** — each region has its own merchant account. For a wrong-region registration, **refund and direct the family to their correct region** (find via ayso.org).

## Donations
- A **background-check donation** may appear during registration (optional). Keep wording broad to avoid creating a restricted donation. Donations are viewable under Financials → Registrations → Donations.
