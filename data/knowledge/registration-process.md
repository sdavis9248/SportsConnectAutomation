# Registration Process — AYSO Region 58 (Fall 2026, PlayMetrics)

## Overview
- Region 58 migrated from SportsConnect (Blue Sombrero) / Sports Affinity to **PlayMetrics** starting the **Fall 2026** season, as part of an AYSO national pilot program.
- Region 58 offers a **Fall program ONLY** — no Winter, Spring, or Summer programs. (One internal setup draft referenced a Spring 19U season; the authoritative parent-facing position is Fall-only. [VERIFY])
- Fall 2026 runs approximately **September through December 2026** (some notes say late August start; 16U/19U start earlier — see schedules-and-season.md).
- **Everyone who registers plays** — there are **no tryouts** for the Core program. All skill levels welcome; no prior experience required ("Everyone Plays").
- Region 58 offers **Core recreational soccer only** — it does **NOT** offer an EXTRA (competitive) program. Refer EXTRA inquiries to another region (e.g., Region 47); each AYSO region operates independently.
- **Open Registration**: there are **no geographic boundaries / home-address restrictions**. Families may register in any region they choose. Division placement is by **birth date**, not address. Most Region 58 families are local to Sherman Oaks / Van Nuys and surrounding areas.
- **No in-person registration** — all registration is online.

## How Parents Register
1. **Returning/imported families**: use the unique **PlayMetrics invitation link** emailed to them (see invitation-links.md). Their account is pre-populated from the data import — they should NOT create a new account.
2. **New families**: register via the **direct registration link** or the website "Register Now" button. Steps: click the registration URL → create an account (email + password) → verify email (sent from `noreply@reg.playmetrics.com`) → enter contact info → add each child's info (name, birthdate, gender) → view and register for eligible programs.
- Only programs matching the player's **age/gender eligibility** automatically appear. A too-young child (no eligible program) will see **"no programs available"** / "NO PROGRAMS AVAILABLE."
- Each program has one or more **packages** (= AYSO divisions); selecting a package shows pricing and any payment options.
- The **PlayMetrics mobile app** works well for registration; the flow mirrors desktop.

## Registration Player Questions (Fall 2026, per child)
Asked once per child, in order:
- **Player Photo** (optional)
- **Preferred name / pronouns** (optional)
- **"Division" dropdown** — auto-populates the player's division based on birth date (parent does not pick); teammate-request and coach-request Yes/No questions are linked ONLY to 6U/7U/8U
- **School Name**
- **Years of Soccer Experience** (None / 1 / 2 / 3 / 4+)
- **Positions Played**
- **"Soccer experience outside Region 58 fall season"** (includes a "None" option)
- **Player Uniform Sizes** (Youth vs Adult), **Jersey Sizes**, **Short Sizes** (full words: Extra Small … Extra Extra Large) — captured for statistical aggregation, not per-player commitment
- **Medical** (Yes/No radio + conditional note field that appears only on "Yes")
- The conditional **"have you played before / provided a birth certificate?"** question was **REMOVED**; the BC upload element is kept (see birth-certificate-verification.md).

## Registration Program Questions (Fall 2026, once per registration session)
- **Emergency Contact** (relationship, name, phone)
- **Volunteer-obligation awareness** Yes/No (a 2× volunteer obligation acknowledgment)

## Waivers
- **AYSO Standards of Conduct** and **AYSO Player & Parent Agreement / Liability Release** — content provided by AYSO National via embedded registration; National (not the region) maintains them.
- A separate region **Refund Policy waiver was removed** — it duplicates the built-in checkout refund agreement.

## Editing Responses After Submission
- A **parent CANNOT change** their own registration responses after submission from the parent side.
- **Admins CAN** view/edit responses: Programs → 2026 Fall Core → Subscriptions list → click the player's subscription → **View Program Responses** → **Edit Responses**.

## Confirmation Emails
- After registration, confirmation emails come from `noreply@ayso58.playmetrics.com` and include all registration responses (player questions, waivers, volunteer selections).

## Common Migration-Period Situations
- **"The website won't let me register"** (while registration is closed): usually means registration is **not yet open** (no available programs), NOT a technical problem. "NO PROGRAMS AVAILABLE" can also mean a division is full.
- A too-young child (born **on or after Aug 1, 2022** — i.e., U4, past the 05U cutoff of Jul 31, 2022) has **no eligible program** and sees nothing available — Region 58 does not offer 4U. (See the DOB chart in divisions-and-programs.md.)
