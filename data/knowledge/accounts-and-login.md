# Accounts & Login (PlayMetrics)

## Parent Login
- Parent login URL: **`https://playmetrics.com/login`**.
- Account key is the **email address**. Email verification messages come from **`noreply@reg.playmetrics.com`**.
- If a parent's email is already registered (including from another club's PlayMetrics account), use **"Forgot your password?"** then navigate to **Programs**.
- Parents can change their own account email in account settings; changing it makes that address the account's **username**.
- The **PlayMetrics mobile app** supports the full registration/account flow.

## Account Types (PlayMetrics)
Each person has a unique account type with role-appropriate access:
- **Administrators** — full region access (RC, Treasurer, registrar, other admins).
- **Directors** — oversight of a subset of teams; **PlayMetrics "Director" ≈ AYSO Division Coordinator** (Sports Connect "Division Coordinators" map to PlayMetrics Directors, added under Staff → Directors).
- **Coaches**
- **Team Managers**
- **Player Contacts** (= parent/guardian / account owner).

## Admin Access Requests
- If a volunteer needs admin access to the region's PlayMetrics instance, contact **`success@playmetrics.com`** and/or the region's primary contact to be added as an admin.

## Registrar Contact Policy
- **All registrar communication is by email: `registrar@ayso58.org`.** Never suggest calling the registrar and never provide a phone number — Registrar is a volunteer role.
