# Financial Aid / Scholarships — Region 58

## How to Apply
- Financial aid / scholarships must be requested **during registration, directly inside the PlayMetrics registration form.** This is the only application channel.
- **Do NOT offer an email-application path.** When replying to a parent who asks about aid, tell them to request it **during registration in the PlayMetrics form** — do not tell them they can apply by replying to the email or by emailing the Registrar.

## Guiding Principle
- AYSO's **"Everyone Plays"** — cost of registration should never prevent a child from participating. Default posture **leans toward approval**.
- Criteria exist to make decisions **consistent and defensible**, not to add scrutiny or tighten access.

## Qualifying Criteria (any ONE qualifies)
- Child(ren) eligible for **Free or Reduced-Price School Lunch**.
- Family receives **SNAP/CalFresh, WIC, Medi-Cal, Section 8, TANF**, or similar assistance.
- **Temporary or ongoing financial hardship** (job loss, medical event, single-parent/deployment, multiple players).
- Families describe their need in the request. A short **narrative is required only** when the basis is "temporary hardship" or "none of the above."
- **NOT an honor-system / automatic approval.** Every request is individually **reviewed**, and the applicant is **informed of the decision**.

## What Aid Covers
- **Local registration fee** ($205): fully or partially waived based on need and available funds.
- **National Player Fee ($25):** Region 58 absorbs it on full waivers so approved families pay nothing out of pocket. [VERIFY — confirm budget supports this]
- **Multi-child households:** aid applies to each registered child individually.

## Process & Timing
- Reviewed within **3–5 business days** by the Registrar in consultation with the Treasurer.
- Reviewed in order received; families encouraged to apply **early** in the registration window.
- **Denials should be rare** — essentially only "no criterion met AND no narrative provided." Respond with a follow-up request for information rather than an outright denial.
- If the season aid budget is fully committed, work with families on **payment plans or partial assistance**.
- PlayMetrics aid tools support **approve/deny** and **percentage discounts** (partial waivers). Decision reasons are documented in PlayMetrics notes for the Treasurer's audit trail.
- The season aid **budget cap is internal** (Registrar/Treasurer) — **do not publish a public dollar cap**.

## Confidentiality
- Financial aid requests are visible **only to the Registrar and Treasurer**.
- Aid status does **not** appear on rosters and is **not** shared with coaches, division coordinators, or other parents.
- Reassure applicants the process is confidential.

## Admin Note
- "Collect financial aid requests during registration" is a **two-gate** toggle (club level AND program level). If aid collection isn't appearing, check Financials → Financial Aid or Account → Settings, or ask PlayMetrics Success to enable. [VERIFY]
