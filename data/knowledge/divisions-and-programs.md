# Divisions & Programs — Region 58 (Fall 2026)

## Program
- Region 58 offers **Core recreational Fall soccer only** (no EXTRA/competitive program; no Spring/Winter/Summer).
- PlayMetrics program name: **2026 Fall Core**.

## Division Placement Policy
- Placement is determined **strictly by date of birth**, using the **August 1 cutoff** (AYSO/U.S. Soccer national standard) — **NOT** simply by birth year and **NOT** by skill level, friendships, school grade, or home address.
- **"U" means "Under"** (e.g., 10U = under 10).
- A child's division is typically a year above their stated age — **do not guess a division from a stated age**; the correct division is determined by birth date and shown at registration. Parents see only **age-eligible** packages during registration (a too-young child sees "no programs available").
- PlayMetrics term: **division = "package."**

## Division Structure
- **Younger divisions are single-year**; **10U and older combine two birth years**; **19U spans three years**. Odd-numbered divisions (9U, 11U, 13U) are skipped to form larger divisions with better competition.
- **Naming formats:** 5U, 6U, 7U, 8U, 10U, 12U, 14U, 16U, 19U, with **B/G** for Boys/Girls (e.g., 06UB, 16UG). Gendered codes are used throughout (Boys and Girls are separate divisions).
- Region 58 does **NOT** offer a **4U** division (children born after Aug 1, 2022 are not served).
- The minimum program offered is **05U**.

## Gender Configuration
- All divisions are **gender-based (separate boys/girls)** EXCEPT **5U "Schoolyard"/Jamboree** (coed) and **19U** (coed).
- **6U is gendered** (per the authoritative Fall 2026 config / age chart). (An older setup draft listed 4U–6U as coed; this is **superseded** — 6U is gendered. [VERIFY if reconfirming for a given season])

## Play Format by Division [VERIFY details]
- **5U Schoolyard/Jamboree:** coed, non-competitive intro; weekly Saturday sessions led by an Advanced Coach, generally scheduled **between 8 AM and noon on Saturdays**, with groups of about **15–20 participants**. **Once a group's session time is set, it stays the same each week** for the season. **No games per se** — participants practice various soccer skills and engage in joint activities; no prior experience needed. **Parents are encouraged to be on the field participating alongside their child.** Volunteering to coach at this level requires **no coaching credentials/license** — it's parent participation, not competitive coaching.
  - **If a parent feels their young player is ready for games:** they can **request a play-up** to 06U (register in the birth-date division first, then submit the play-up form — see Play-Up/Play-Down below; not guaranteed). Set expectations: even **06U has "games," but with no referees and no goalkeepers — the coaches act as referees.** It's still instructional, not competitive officiated play.
- **6U:** instructional — practice then game, both on Saturdays; 6-player teams, 4v4, ~25-min games; no goalkeepers/throw-ins; **no scorekeeping**; coaches act as referees.
- **7U:** adds weekday practices; 4v4 on 45×30 yd field; no goalkeepers; no scorekeeping. (AYSO groups 7U/8U for coach training — only 8U training exists.)
- **8U:** 5v5; introduces throw-ins; no scorekeeping.
- **10U:** introduces goalkeepers, **competitive play, scorekeeping/standings**; 7v7 on 75×40 yd field; playoffs.
- **12U:** 9v9 on 90–100 yd fields, full-size goals, positional play.
- **14U:** 11v11 full-size fields, 60-min games, min ¾ participation.
- **16U:** shortened ~6-week season; games Saturdays and Sundays at regional parks.
- **19U:** formed if enough players register; shortened season; often plays at other parks.

## Team Formation (Parent-Facing)
- **Instructional divisions (8U and younger):** teammate and coach requests are **accepted during registration**, but the **Division Manager forms teams**; coaches volunteer and do **NOT** pick their own rosters. A **single pairing** may be accommodated; larger groups are less likely to all be placed together.
- **Competitive divisions (10U and older):** teams are formed by **coach draft for balanced teams** — coach and teammate requests **CANNOT** be accommodated.

## Play-Up / Play-Down Requests (Sara's Policy)
- Families must **FIRST register in the division assigned by birth date**. To request a change, complete the Google Form **AFTER** registering: `https://bit.ly/2026playerrequest`.
- **No request is guaranteed** to be approved — replies must state this clearly.
- **Do NOT** direct parents to contact the Commissioner directly — all requests go through the form regardless of who they are. The Commissioner reviews requests with each Division Coordinator.
- Players near the cutoff asking to play up/down may also be directed to registrar@ayso58.org, but the form is the required path.

## EXTRA / Competitive Inquiries
- Region 58 offers **Core recreational Fall only**. Refer EXTRA inquiries to another region (e.g., Region 47), noting that regions operate independently.
