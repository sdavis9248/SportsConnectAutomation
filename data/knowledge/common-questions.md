# Common Parent Questions — Region 58 (Quick Answers)

## "When does registration open?"
- Welcome them; explain Region 58 offers a **Fall-only program**; give the expected open timeline (registration was migrating to a new system this year — opened around **May 2026**; one source cites May 1, another ~May 15 [VERIFY]). Advise creating an account at **ayso58.org** to be notified when registration opens. Thank them for patience during the system migration.
- **There are no waitlists or pre-registration lists** before registration opens — just create an account to be notified.

## "The website won't let me register."
- While registration is closed: explain it **hasn't opened yet** (that's why they can't register), not a technical issue. Assume they likely already have an account; mention account creation only as a fallback. Reassure they'll be notified when it opens.
- **"NO PROGRAMS AVAILABLE"** can also mean a division is **full**, or the child is **too young** for any offered division (Region 58 starts at 05U; no 4U).

## "I didn't get the invitation email."
- Check status (registered vs imported-not-registered); **resend the invitation**; tell them to check **spam** for `noreply@playmetrics.com`; provide the **direct registration link**. If their email is already registered (possibly via another club), have them log in at `playmetrics.com/login` and use "Forgot your password?". (Full detail: invitation-links.md.)

## "Should I create a new account?" (returning/imported family)
- **No.** Use the **invitation link** already emailed — the account is pre-populated. Creating a new account orphans the imported record and forces a BC re-upload.

## "What division will my child be in?"
- Do **not** guess from a stated age. Placement is by **date of birth** (Aug 1 cutoff) and the correct division **appears at registration**. Only state a specific division if confirmed from registration data. (Detail: divisions-and-programs.md.)

## "Can my child play up / down?"
- They must **first register** in the birth-date division, then complete the play-up/down form: `https://bit.ly/2026playerrequest`. **No request is guaranteed.** Do not refer them to the Commissioner directly — all requests go through the form. (Detail: divisions-and-programs.md.)

## "Can my child be placed with a friend/teammate, or with a specific coach?"
- **Depends on the division.** Teammate and coach requests are **only accommodated in the instructional divisions (8U and younger)** — and even there the Division Manager forms teams (a single pairing is more likely than a whole group).
- **In the competitive divisions (10U and older), we do NOT accommodate teammate or coach requests** — teams are formed by a balanced coach draft. If a 10U+ family asks, politely explain we're unable to honor teammate/coach requests at that level because teams are drafted for balance. (Detail: divisions-and-programs.md.)

## "Do you have an EXTRA / competitive / club program?"
- Region 58 offers **Core recreational Fall soccer only**. Refer EXTRA inquiries to another region (e.g., Region 47); regions operate independently.

## "Is there a Spring season?"
- **No** — Region 58 offers a **Fall program only** (no Winter, Spring, or Summer).

## "How much does it cost?"
- **$205 regional fee + $25 National Player Fee = $230 per player.** The early-bird discount has **ended for 2026** (no longer available). **No multi-child/sibling discount** — each child pays full price. No late fees, no payment plans. The $25 NPF is non-refundable. (Detail: fees-payments-refunds.md.)

## "Can I get financial aid / a scholarship?"
- Yes — it must be requested **during registration, directly in the PlayMetrics registration form** (the only way to apply; do **not** offer an email-application path). It's **confidential**. Each request is **reviewed and the family is informed of the decision** — do **not** describe it as honor-system, automatic, or guaranteed. (Detail: financial-aid.md.)

## "I want to coach / volunteer."
- Sign up during registration (Head Coach, Assistant Coach, Team Manager, Referee, Board Member) or afterward via the **Coach Request Link**. Returning credentials not showing yet is **expected** during the pilot and will sync later. (Detail: volunteers-and-coaching.md.)

## "Do I need to be a certified coach for 05U?"
- **No.** 05U (Schoolyard/Jamboree) has **no games per se** — it's a coed, non-competitive intro where participants practice various soccer skills and engage in joint activities, and **parents are encouraged to be on the field participating with their child.** Helping/coaching at 05U requires **no coaching license or credentials**. Encourage interested parents to jump in. (Detail: divisions-and-programs.md / volunteers-and-coaching.md.)

## "Am I waitlisted / will my child get to play?"
- AYSO's philosophy is **"Everyone Plays."** Register promptly since divisions can fill (capacity depends on players + available head coaches); there's a free waitlist. **19U is waitlist-only** by design. Only state a specific waitlist status if confirmed from a data lookup. (Detail: waitlist.md.)

## "I registered in the wrong region."
- Payments **cannot transfer between regions** (each has its own merchant account). The region will **refund** and direct the family to their correct region (find it via **ayso.org**).

## "When/where are games and practices?"
- **14U and younger:** Saturday games at **Van Nuys Sherman Oaks Memorial Park** (~1 hr, scheduled within 8 AM–3 PM). **7U+** also have weekday practices (coach-set, ~1–2×/week). **5U/6U:** Saturdays only. **16U:** Sundays at Region 174 (Granada Hills). (Detail: schedules-and-season.md.)

## Postseason ($25 fee appears) — STOP gotcha
- All-Star/Tournament registration is **invitation-only and free**. If a **$25 "AYSO Membership Fee"** appears, the family should **STOP** and email registrar@ayso58.org — the system isn't recognizing the player as a Fall participant. (Detail: all-star-and-tournament.md.)
