# Volunteers & Coaching — Region 58 (Fall 2026)

## Signing Up to Volunteer
- Volunteers can sign up **during player registration** (checkboxes: **Head Coach, Assistant Coach, Team Manager, Referee, Board Member** — no "Other") OR **after registration**.
- **Head Coach** selection collects coach contact info inline.
- The **volunteer discount is OFF** (do not enable — volunteers are not paid).

## Coach Signup After Registration — Coach Request Link
- Use the **Coach Request Link** — a **GENERAL (not per-person) link**, the same URL for everyone. Parents click it, log in, and it connects the coaching request to their player.
- Get/share it from **Leagues → Coaching Requests**.
- Region 58 Coach Request Link: `https://playmetrics.com/signup?clubToken=...&coach_request_league_id=11427` [VERIFY]
- The standalone link can be placed on the website; arriving via it requires login (creates a PlayMetrics account).
- Admins can also add a volunteer position via the player's subscription (View Program Responses → Edit Responses) or add/edit the coaching request under Leagues → Coaching Requests.

## Region 58 Coaching Request Questions
- Coached AYSO before? / age groups coached
- AYSO coaching licenses held (6U/8U/10U/12U/Intermediate/Advanced)
- Coaching roles able to fill (HC/AC)
- **Division preference** (multi-select all 17 divisions — lets a coach request a division different from their child's; exports as comma-separated)
- Training-requirements acknowledgment (incl. California fingerprinting)
- Coach jersey need + gender style + size
- Conditional/dependent questions render only after the triggering selection.

## 05U (Schoolyard/Jamboree) Coaching — No Credentials Needed
- 05U has **no games per se** — it's a coed, non-competitive intro where participants practice various soccer skills and engage in joint activities. **Parents are encouraged to come onto the field and participate alongside their child.**
- Volunteering to help/coach at 05U requires **no coaching license or credentials** — reassure interested 05U parents that no experience or certification is needed and encourage them to jump in.
- (Compliance certifications still apply to anyone formally registered as a tracked Head/Assistant Coach, but for 05U the message to parents is: just come participate — you don't need to be a credentialed coach.)

## "My Returning Credentials Don't Show in PlayMetrics"
- **This is expected.** The PlayMetrics **Governing System** (background checks / AYSOU certifications) is **not yet connected** for pilot regions. Existing credentials are **safe** and will sync later. Returning volunteers continue through the existing Sports Affinity / eTrainU pipeline in the interim.

# AYSO Volunteer Compliance Requirements (Policy Reference)

## Roles Tracked for Compliance
- Compliance tracking applies to **four roles only: Head Coach, Assistant Coach, Referee, Youth Referee.** All other roles (Team Parent, Manager, board roles) are untracked.

## Required Certifications by Role
All tracked volunteers need: **AYSO Safe Haven, Concussion Awareness, Sudden Cardiac Arrest, Risk Status (background check).** Additionally:
- **Head Coach / Assistant Coach + Referee:** **CA Mandated Fingerprinting + SafeSport.**
- **Coaches:** an **age-appropriate Coach License** matching their division (e.g., a 10U division requires at least a 10U-level license; a lower license is "Insufficient License"). For **14U+**: also the **14U Coach Pre-Course Module (Parts 1 & 2)** and **Summary of Laws of the Game** (100% attendance). [VERIFY]
- **Referees:** a **Referee grade/certification** plus the **Regional Referee Online Course** and the **Regional Referee Companion Course** (in-person). [VERIFY]
- **Youth Referees are EXEMPT from SafeSport and Fingerprinting.** Required set: Safe Haven, Concussion, Cardiac, Risk Status, and Referee Grade only. Minimum age **12** [VERIFY].

## Certification Verification States
- **Verified:** the certification's Verified field = 'Y'.
- **Pending Verification:** Verified = 'N' but the volunteer has actually completed it (valid/current/future upload or expire date). Valid but admin still needs to verify it. **Flag for admin action; do NOT include in reminder emails to volunteers.**
- **Missing:** Verified = 'N' AND no valid date (blank or expired) → needs volunteer to complete/renew.
- **SafeSport gotcha:** If SafeSport Verified = 'N' AND the expire date is blank, treat as **Missing** (not Pending). Pending only when there's a current/future expire date.
- **Concussion & Sudden Cardiac Arrest special case:** treat as **Pending Verification** whenever Verified = 'N', regardless of dates.
- Pending counts are a **subset** of the total non-compliant count.

## Risk Status (Background Check)
Risk Status reflects the AYSO national background check (in PlayMetrics = JDP Background Check Status):
- **Green – Eligible:** any position.
- **Blue – Youth Volunteers:** referees / EPIC buddies / supervised positions only; **may NOT** coach or be a voting board member; valid only for youth volunteers.
- **Yellow – Restricted from Coaching:** not coach/assistant coach; OK other positions.
- **Orange – Restricted from Funds:** not for fund/PII roles; OK other positions.
- **Brown – Restricted from Coaching AND Funds.**
- **Red – Ineligible:** no position (usually expires 5–20 years out).
- **Gray – Pending:** under review / consent not completed / needs Safe Haven review. **Must NOT be assigned any role until cleared.** Questions: **cvpa@ayso.org**.
- **None/No Status:** consent form not completed.
- **DISQUALIFIED/INELIGIBLE:** never eligible, no end date.

### Compliance by Role
- **Coaches (Head/Assistant):** acceptable = **Green, Orange**. Blocked = Yellow, Brown, Red, Gray, Expired, None.
- **Referees:** acceptable = **Green, Yellow, Orange, Brown**. Blocked = Red, Gray, Expired, None.
- **Youth Referees:** acceptable = **Green, Blue, Yellow, Orange, Brown**. Blocked = Red, Gray, Expired, None.
- **Expired** counts as a compliance item needing attention. **Gray/Pending** is treated as Pending Verification (admin review).

## Compliance Tiers (being finalized by AYSO National)
- **Tier 1 (full clearance):** background check + SafeSport + job-specific training (e.g., 10U+ coaches/referees).
- **Tier 2 (reduced):** background check + job training (e.g., 6U/8U where parents are on-field).
- **Tier 3 (youth):** exempt until 18th birthday.
